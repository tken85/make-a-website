About My Cooking

Up until 2011 I couldn't cook anything not requiring a microwave. I even once had a friend cook a romantic Valentine's Day meal for me and my then girlfriend. But things changed when I moved in with my sister after graduate school and needed to find something to do to keep me busy during the job hunt when The Price is Right wasn't on. I started learning how to cook vegetarian for her. Then I quickly developed a love for baking, and eventually even branched out to learning how to cook meat once I moved back to Texas.
